About

Hi, my name is Zach Denton. I’m currently studying at Dartmouth and building apps like a collaborative music-making platform, an intelligent online publishing system for organizations, and something that will ensure you never again lose a game of checkers.

I’m interested in music, neuroengineering, long-term travel, metacognition, artificial intelligence, autodidacticism, and all kinds of creative recreation. I also enjoy snowboarding, mountain biking, tennis, roasting coffee, and urban exploration.
